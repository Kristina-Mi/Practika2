RETURN POLICY

Return Policy

The product is of good quality

The product is of good quality without specifying the reasons (if the product was not in use, factory labels, presentation, consumer properties, etc. are preserved), you can return it within 14 calendar days, not counting the day of purchase.

According to Article 26.1. "Remote method of selling goods" of the Law "On Consumer Protection", all categories of goods ordered through the online store are subject to return. The cost of the delivery service, as well as postage for sending the goods to the warehouse of the online store, are non-refundable.

If you have received an order at a retail store, then you can return the goods of proper quality only to the Befree retail store (return to the store of the franchise network is not possible). The status of the store can be found on the page https://befree.ru/stores . You must have the product with you, as well as a receipt or other confirmation of payment for the order.

IF you have received an order at the PVZ, the Russian Post office or by courier delivery, then you can return the goods of proper quality to the retail store (if available in your locality) or to the warehouse of the online store by mail. For this:

1. Carefully pack the product in its original packaging in full (the goods supplied in the package should also be returned in the package);

2. Fill out the return application Form Befree.pdf attached to the order;

3. Attach a copy of the cash receipt, specify the method of refund (postal transfer or transfer to a bank account with the details for the transfer);

4. Send the parcel by mail to the address: 200961, Sofiyskaya str., 81 litera r. 1, A/Z No. 83. Recipient: JSC "Malon Fashion Group" (without cash on delivery, registered parcel).

An item purchased in an online store can be exchanged for a similar one through the return procedure and making a new purchase.

Eligibility

Process

Refund

Expect a refund to your original payment method within 7-10 days.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Cancellation Policy

The product of inadequate quality (defective)

The product of inadequate quality (defective) with any method of order delivery, you can return it to the retail store if it is available in your locality. At the time of checking the quality of the product and completing the refund procedure, the store has the right to take the goods for storage against the receipt of an authorized employee.

In case of refusal to issue a refund after a visual inspection, the customer has the right to file a claim in the store, the claim review period is 10 days.

To request a refund of the cost of the delivery service in case of a marriage, please contact the post office info@befree.ru .

If there is no retail store in your city, then you can return the goods of inadequate quality to the warehouse of the online store. For this:

1. Carefully pack the product in its original packaging in full (the goods supplied in the package should also be returned in the package);

2. Fill out the return application Form Befree.pdf attached to the order;

3. Attach a copy of the cash receipt, specify the method of refund (postal transfer or transfer to a bank account with the details for the transfer);

4. Send the parcel by mail to the address: 200961, Sofiyskaya str., 81 litera r. 1, A/Z No. 83. Recipient: JSC "Malon Fashion Group" (without cash on delivery, registered parcel).

If you received the wrong product that you ordered, then you can return it only to the warehouse of the online store (by mail). For this:

1. Carefully pack the product in its original packaging in full (the goods supplied in the package should also be returned in the package);

2. Fill out the return application Form Befree.pdf attached to the order;

3. Attach a copy of the cash receipt, specify the method of refund (postal transfer or transfer to a bank account with the details for the transfer);

4. Send the parcel by mail to the address: 200961, Sofiyskaya str., 81 litera r. 1, A/Z No. 83. Recipient: JSC "Malon Fashion Group" (without cash on delivery, registered parcel).
